Massage Angels

Massage Angels will ensure that you team are made to feel energised, relaxed and rejuvenated.  Inviting massage angels to your workplace is the ultimate way to relax, reward and revive your team!

What are the benefits of angel massage?

  • It melts away your team’s stresses;
  • It rejuvenates;
  • It makes your staff more motivated and productive;
  • It is an amazing way to reward your team and recognise their efforts by offering them massage at work;
  • It increases team morale, enthusiasm and energy; and
  • It helps your staff cope with looming deadlines by reducing stress levels and anxiety.

Corporate Care Therapies Massage Angels can come to your office – anywhere in Australia (Including Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth, Sydney). Our angels can set up in a quiet room, equipped with relaxing music, or roam from desk to desk or bring our seated massage chairs.

Our angels perform deeply relaxing corporate massages at work, but understand that once finished, your team needs to get back to their work. All that undergo a corporate massage will feel lively and refreshed, ready to tackle their next big task.

There are two massage angel options to choose from:

  • Desk massages: This involves the angel offering a short massage at employee’s desks. The angel will massage each person’s back, shoulders, neck, head, arms and hands. Recommended massage time is 10 minutes per person, but can be shorter on request.
  • Seated massage: This involves the angel bringing their massage chair and providing a massage to the recipient’s back, shoulders, neck, arms and hands. For best therapeutic results we ask the company to allocate a separate room where the angel can set up their equipment and play relaxing music. Recommended massage time is 15 or 20 minutes per person.

Please note: All massages carried out by our massage angels require individuals to be fully clothed. We do not use oil to perform a deeply relaxing and rejuvenating massage.

Did you know?

On-site / At-desk massage corporate health sessions are the most effective intervention for decreasing the duration of musculoskeletal aches, pains or discomfort, and for increasing range of motion.
